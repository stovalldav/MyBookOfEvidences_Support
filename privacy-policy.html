Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MyBookOfEvidences ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

No Data Collection: MyBookOfEvidences is designed with privacy as a priority. All your journal entries, images, and personal settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information or journal content on our servers.

No Analytics or Tracking: We do not implement any third-party analytics or tracking tools in our app.

Local Data Storage

All data created in the App, including:

  • Journal entries and their content
  • Images added to entries
  • Custom themes and settings

is stored exclusively on your device. This data is not transmitted to us or any third parties unless you explicitly choose to export and share it.

Security Measures

While all data stays on your device, we've implemented several security features to help you protect your journal:

  • Password protection option
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID)

These security features are optional and can be configured in the app's settings.

Data Export

The app allows you to export your journal entries as PDF documents. Please be aware that once you export your data, it's your responsibility to keep those exports secure.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
